# Monitors

Quasarr's monitoring system keeps you updated on the latest developments in crypto and blockchain.

## How Monitors Work

Monitors scan various sources in real time and send alerts to designated Discord channels. You receive instant notifications when something relevant happens.

## Available Monitors

### Tweet Scanner
Captures and analyzes important tweets from key accounts in the crypto space. Get notified when influential accounts post about projects or market events.

### NFT Ordinals Monitor
Tracks new and trending NFT and Ordinals projects. See what collections are gaining attention.

### GameFi Monitor
Keeps an eye on the latest developments in blockchain gaming. New game launches, updates and partnerships.

### Token Monitor
Alerts you to new token launches and significant token movements. Track what tokens are being created.

### DApps & Protocols Monitor
Follows the latest in decentralized applications and protocols. New deployments and major updates.

### Potential Gems Monitor
Identifies promising projects with high growth potential. Early stage projects worth watching.

### Rising Projects Monitor
Tracks projects gaining traction in the community. See what is trending before it goes mainstream.

### Runes Monitor
Tracks newly minted runes in real time. Get instant updates on new rune creations.

### Investment Monitor
Tracks significant funding events and investments in the blockchain space. Know when projects receive major funding.

### Meme Monitor
Scans for memes and viral events. Stay updated on crypto culture and trends.

### Pump.fun Monitor
Tracks tokens created on Pump.fun that relate to significant tweets. Catch potential opportunities early.

## Notification Settings

Channel admins can configure which monitors send alerts to specific channels. Check with your server admin for current settings.
