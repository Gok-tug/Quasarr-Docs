# Runes Monitor

Tracks newly minted Runes in real time and continuously updates the information. It also sends notifications when specific conditions are met regarding these Runes.

## What It Tracks

The Runes Monitor watches for:
- New rune mints on Bitcoin
- Significant trading activity
- Notable price movements
- Large holder accumulation

## Notifications

When a new rune meets the criteria, you receive a notification with:
- Rune name and details
- Mint information
- Relevant links
