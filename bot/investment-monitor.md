# Investment Monitor

Tracks investments made by significant funds and provides detailed notifications about these investments.

## What It Tracks

The Investment Monitor follows:
- Venture capital funding rounds
- Major fund investments
- Significant funding events in blockchain space

## Notifications

When a notable investment is detected, you receive:
- Project name and details
- Investment amount
- Participating funds
- Links for further research

## Why It Matters

Following smart money can help you identify promising projects early. When major funds invest in a project, it often signals confidence in the team and technology.
