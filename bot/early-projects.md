# Early Projects Monitor

Quasarr's Early Project Monitors are designed to give you a competitive edge by identifying and tracking promising projects in their nascent stages. These specialized monitors cover various sectors of the blockchain and crypto ecosystem, ensuring you're always ahead of the curve.

## How It Works

Early Projects Monitors provide alerts to their respective roles in Discord. These notifications cover the following categories:

- **NFT Ordinals** - New and trending NFT and Ordinals projects
- **GameFi** - Blockchain gaming developments
- **Token** - New token launches and movements
- **DApps & Protocols** - Decentralized applications updates
- **Potential Gems** - Projects with high growth potential
- **Rising Projects** - Projects gaining community traction

## Notification Example

When a new project is detected, you receive a notification with key details about the project, its category and relevant links for further research.
